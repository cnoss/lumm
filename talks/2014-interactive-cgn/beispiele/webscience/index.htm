First Web Science Summer School in Southampton, UK

The University of Southampton will host the first Web Science Summer School this year from 20. to 26. July. You will get the chance to listen and talk to the invited speakers Wendy Hall, Jim Hendler, Cory Doctorow, Chris Welty, and Guus Schreiber and network with other Masters and PhD students.

See more information regarding the programme, the speakers and tutors here: http://summerschool.websci.net/

The summer school requires a registration fee (€ 670). Registration is possible until 13.06.2014.

Deadline for International Applicants approaching –
apply NOW!

The deadline for international applications for the upcoming winter term ends on June 30th. Since the application process via uni assist takes some time, the sooner you submit your documents, the better. The first on-site weekend in winter term will take place on 20.-22. September 2013.

Students with foreign certificates have to apply via Uni-Assist. Please take also a look at theHelp-Site of Uni-Assist and follow every step. This is very important, since otherwise some important steps might be forgotten and will then hinder the application process. Unfortunately there is not that much time left, so if you want to apply please make sure, that you

  • upload all important documents at once to uni assist
  • send the certificates also paper-based
  • pay the uni-assist fee

Otherwise uni-assist won’t proceed your application.
