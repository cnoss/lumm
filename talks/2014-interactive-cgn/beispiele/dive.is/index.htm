DIving & Snorkeling in Iceland

It has been our pleasure taking scuba divers and snorkelers from all over the world to some of Iceland’s and our planet´s most unique and amazing dive and snorkel sites since 1997.

Our mission is to offer diving & snorkelling as well as other related services of the highest standards while being a socially & environmentally aware company.

To offer you a safe, fun, educational and responsible dive/snorkel experience that stands out in your memory is what our people strive & succeed to create every single day.

Dive & Snorkel Sites in Iceland

Iceland has some of the most amazing and unique dive & snorkel sites on this planet

The fresh water fissures Silfra, Davidsgjá, & Nésgjá exhibit the continental divide in its clearest form. Strýtan, the geothermal chimney, the underwater hot springs of Lake Kleifarvatn and the heated river Litlaá offer absolutely unique diving surrounded by geothermal activity... ... and the Ocean is full of life!

Dive Course done in Iceland

Take advantage of learning to dive in a dry suit

We offer the whole range of PADI Courses, starting with Discover Scuba programs to becoming a PADI Staff Instructor and everything in between.

The PADI Dry Suit Course and our Tectonic Awareness Specialty Course we teach at least once a week.

We especially recommend that you become a PADI Dry Suit Diver before doing one of our dive tours.
